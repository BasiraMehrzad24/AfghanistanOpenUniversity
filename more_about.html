Afghanistan Open University

World Class Free Higher Education for All.

Background:

Afghanistan Open University (AOU) is an Afghanistan-based registered (registration is in the process), nonprofit, nonpolitical, non-sectorial, and world-class higher education provider institution. It is established to address all the challenges that the higher education system currently faces in Afghanistan.

Every nation dreams of a world-class free education for its citizens. Incontrovertibly, it is higher education that decides a nation’s future. A dynamic, free, and modern higher education system surely plays a promising role in a nation’s sovereignty in political, economic, industrial, and all other aspects. On the other hand, a stagnated education system leads to the deterioration of a nation and impacts all aspects of individual and collective life. Even before the introduction of a modern higher education system, Afghanistan had been home to many elite academics, scientists, professors, and poets. Soon after laying the foundation of modern education in the 1930s, Afghanistan was a country with a well￾developed and world-class higher education system. Decades of war and invasions have not only left behind a dysfunctional economy and a war-torn nation, but also the world’s weakest higher education system, which is further deteriorated intellectually by the brain drain caused by the weak economy as Afghan scholars are leaving the country. The war has prevented the higher education system of Afghanistan from growing to align with today’s international standards.

It should be the prime duty of a nation and its government to provide every one of its dwellers with free and standard education. Regrettably, the Afghan government and nation have so far failed to equip its citizens with world-class free higher education and it is one of the main causes of all the problems that the nation has been facing so far. Therefore for the first time, a self-motivated team of educated youths, educators, and community leaders are committed to launching and running the country’s first non-governmental tuition-free university. To adequately counter all the challenges (such as finances, and shortage of educators) that the higher education system has been facing since its establishment. AOU’s team has come up with a public-driven design initiative called “Afghanistan Open University” the first non-governmental tuition-free university in Afghanistan, powered by the Hybrid-Blended Learning mode.

The initiative is strongly believed to be not only quite adequate against the inherited problems of the Afghan higher education system but fully applicable in technologically and financially lacking environments such as Afghanistan. Free higher education with international standards will not only have a huge impact in terms of adding values and uplifting quality of life, but it will also generate an impressive positive impact on the mindset of Afghans and in particular Afghan youths.

Our Philosophy:

The philosophy of the University is built upon the importance of seeking, utilizing and sharing knowledge in Islam. Islam obliges it as a religious and supreme duty upon Muslims (males and females). The first word revealed from Allah is Iqrah “to read”, which means to educate humans (males and females). If a glance is paid to the first five verses revealed from the Holy Qur’an, we can witness that the word “Iqrah”, (to read) is iterated twice, the word “Qalam”, (pen) is mentioned once and the word “Alama”, (to teach) is called twice as below: “Read! in the name of your Lord, Who created: Created man from a clot [of blood]. Read! Your Lord is the Most Generous One. Who taught by the pen, Taught man what he knew not.” (Qur’an 96:1-5 ) The importance of acquiring, utilizing, and sharing knowledge is considered the two most supreme values of “Ibadah” (continuous worship) and “Amanah” (a trust) placed on humans by Allah (SWT).

Our Vision:

To be a world-class research university known for its diversity, knowledge, research, innovation, and international collaboration.

Our Mission:

Shaping and strengthening communities by promoting free education, training, research, technology, and international collaboration to nurture a generation with high morals and well-equipped with contemporary innovative technologies so that they are able to think independently and innovate sustainable solutions even beyond their areas of study to contribute to socio-economic development and moral upliftment of the society; thereby also cultivating in our students to treat leaning as “Ibadah” (continuous worshiping of Allah SWT) and utilizing it as “Amanah” (a trust from Allah SWT).

Goals and Objectives:

  • Improving the quality of life by utilizing world-class education for everyone through the world's most effective and contemporary learning approaches for free.
  • Nurturing growth, and academic excellence to uncover sustainable solutions for emerging societal challenges by utilizing the findings of the research, innovation, and collaboration in the light of Islamic principles and social ethics.
  • Establishing a modern, internationally connected education system and bringing global expertise, research, knowledge, and experiences home; enabling the students to exchange knowledge, expertise, research, and innovation around the globe.
